How Linear Model Works

Travel (min) ≈ w·x + b — features: time-of-day, distance, speed, dwell (normalized). Training targets follow physics: (distance ÷ speed) × 60 + (dwellsec ÷ 60).

Inference uses the learned linear weights; synthetic data keeps weights aligned with kinematic travel time for stable ETAs.
